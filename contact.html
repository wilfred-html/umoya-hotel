Getting here

Umoya is tucked into a quiet residential pocket of Hout Bay — close enough to everything, yet peacefully removed. If you’d like, we can recommend trusted transfer options.

  • From Cape Town CBD: approx. 25–35 minutes (traffic dependent).
  • From Cape Town International Airport (CPT): approx. 35–55 minutes.
  • Best scenic route: take Chapman's Peak Drive for one of the most beautiful coastal drives in the world.
  • Parking: available on/near the property (please confirm on booking).
