Welcome to Umoya

A breath of fresh air in the heart of Hout Bay

Nestled between the dramatic peaks of Chapman's Peak and the azure waters of Hout Bay, Umoya is more than a place to stay — it's a sanctuary where time slows, the air is pure, and every detail has been crafted with intention. Here, African warmth embraces refined luxury, creating an intimate escape unlike any other in Cape Town.
