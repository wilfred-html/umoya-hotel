Welcome

A breath of fresh air in the heart of Hout Bay

Inspired by the Zulu and Xhosa word for 'air and breath,' Umoya captures the invigorating sea breeze from the nearby bay, creating a seamless flow of fresh air that caresses your skin.

Guests are treated to endless views and spectacular sunsets across the bay, with the iconic Chapman's Peak Drive and the vast Atlantic Ocean stretching before them.
