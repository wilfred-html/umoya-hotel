Experiences

Farm to Table

Eat With Us

Breakfast is served from 7am to 10am daily, comprising fresh produce sourced from our garden and local farmers. Guests choose between indoor dining, the terrace overlooking Hout Bay, or the shaded courtyard.

Heated Pool

Swim With Us

Our 10-metre heated pool is positioned for sweeping views of the bay. Wide, generous steps and a gently sloped floor make it perfect for lounging, exercise, or simply taking in the balmy evenings.

Rest & Renewal

Relax With Us

Our well-stocked library and roaring fireplace provide the perfect escape. Take our dogs for a walk through indigenous gardens, or book an in-room treatment from our network of massage and bodywork therapists.

Beyond Umoya

Explore With Us

Hout Bay sits on Cape Town's scenic Blue Route, just 20 minutes from the city centre. The area offers:

  • Hiking trails for every fitness level
  • Cycling routes and scenic bike tours
  • Wine tasting at estates 5 minutes away
  • Chapman's Peak and Table Mountain trails
  • Gorgeous beaches and water sports
  • Mariner's Wharf restaurants and shops
