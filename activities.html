Nourishment

Eat With Us

Every morning from 7 to 10am, our terrace comes alive with the aromas of farm-to-table breakfast. We source ingredients from our own gardens and local farms, creating dishes that celebrate the bounty of the Cape.

Dine indoors in our elegant breakfast room, or choose the outdoor terrace with sweeping views over Hout Bay. Watch the morning light paint the mountains while you savor freshly baked bread, seasonal fruit, farm eggs, and locally roasted coffee.

What to Expect

  • Breakfast served 7:00am - 10:00am daily
  • Ingredients from our gardens and local farms
  • Indoor and outdoor seating options
  • Panoramic views over Hout Bay
  • Accommodates dietary restrictions (advance notice appreciated)
  • Coffee and tea available throughout the day

Rejuvenation

Swim With Us

Our heated 10-meter pool is more than a place to swim — it's a meditation in water. With panoramic views over Hout Bay, wide accessible steps, and perfectly maintained temperature year-round, the pool becomes whatever you need it to be.

Morning lap swimmer? The sunrise over the bay is your reward. Afternoon floater? Let the mountain shadows cool the day. Evening contemplator? Watch the lights of Hout Bay twinkle to life as you drift.

Pool Features

  • 10-meter heated pool
  • Maintained at comfortable temperature year-round
  • Panoramic bay and mountain views
  • Wide steps for easy access
  • Wheelchair accessible
  • Loungers and shaded areas
  • Towels provided

Restoration

Relax With Us

Sometimes the best experience is simply... being. Our library invites you to curl up with a book by the fireplace. Our gardens welcome you to wander among indigenous plants. Tamba and Winston are always ready for a gentle walk.

For deeper restoration, we can arrange in-room massage and bodywork with skilled local therapists. Choose from Swedish massage, deep tissue work, reflexology, or specialized treatments designed to melt away travel fatigue and reconnect you with your body.

Relaxation Options

  • Library with fireplace and curated book collection
  • Indigenous gardens for peaceful walks
  • Dog walks with Tamba and Winston (our gentle ambassadors)
  • In-room massage and bodywork (advance booking required)
  • Swedish, deep tissue, and reflexology treatments available
  • Quiet spaces throughout the property
  • Yoga mats available upon request

Adventure

Explore With Us

Hout Bay is your gateway to some of Cape Town's most spectacular experiences. Chapman's Peak Drive — one of the world's most scenic coastal roads — is minutes away. Table Mountain, beaches, wine estates, and water sports are all within easy reach.

We're happy to help arrange everything from hiking guides to wine tasting tours, cycling routes to boat charters. Or simply ask us for our favorite local spots — the hidden beach, the best sunset viewpoint, the family-run restaurant that tourists never find.

Nearby Experiences

  • Chapman's Peak Drive (iconic scenic route)
  • Hiking trails for all levels
  • Cycling routes through stunning landscapes
  • Wine tasting (estates just 5 minutes away)
  • Beaches for swimming, surfing, and relaxation
  • Mariner's Wharf for fresh seafood and harbor views
  • Table Mountain (cable car and hiking access)
  • Water sports: kayaking, surfing, boat charters
  • Local markets and artisan studios
